# LivingCityEngine Game Design Document

## Vision

LivingCityEngine is an original city-building simulation experience focused on creating beautiful, living worlds where every decision affects citizens, neighborhoods, and the future of the city.

The goal is to combine creativity, strategy, and emotional connection.

---

# Core Fantasy

The player is the creator and leader of a growing city.

They begin with a small settlement and transform it into a thriving living world.

The player should feel:

- Creative
- Responsible
- Connected
- Proud of their city

---

# Gameplay Loop

The main gameplay loop:

1. Build
2. Attract citizens
3. Manage resources
4. Solve problems
5. Expand
6. Improve quality of life

---

# City Growth

Cities evolve through stages:

## Settlement

Small beginning community.

Features:

- Basic homes
- Roads
- Local services


## Town

Growing population.

Features:

- Businesses
- Schools
- Parks
- Transportation


## City

Complex urban environment.

Features:

- Districts
- Advanced services
- Tourism
- Industry


## Metropolis

Large living simulation.

Features:

- Advanced economy
- Major infrastructure
- Regional influence

# Living City Philosophy

Buildings are not just objects.

They exist because citizens need them.

Citizens create stories through:

- Work
- Travel
- Relationships
- Events
- Decisions
